MIT App Inventor Project

PLTW Computer Science Principles

This project was made on MIT App Inventor. The goal was to create an app based off of an existing one and customize it. The app we made is called "Where's my Car". When you park your car, you have to click on "save location" and your location and coordinates will save. When you want to return to your car again, you just can click on "Show Directions" and it leads to Google Maps and directions. You can click on the help button if you are confused about something. Clicking on the red marker leads you to a live map to show your location. You can choose whether you want the satellite mode on or off. When we were making this app, we learned about how to use location sensors.
